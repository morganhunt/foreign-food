Appetizer

Chicken Spring Rolls

While in a traditional Thai meal, appetizers are more like smaller entres brought out to share among a family during the rest of the meal, foods such as the spring roll can be easily used as an appetizer that Western countries are more familiar with. Spring rolls with a dipping sauce would be a delicious light starter for the meal.

Entre

Pad Thai

This entre is a dish of noodles, made with shrimp, beef, pork, chicken or tofu depending on preferance. It's flavored with peanuts and often spiced to taste--so when making it, be aware of the heat you can eat. A recipe made with shrimp can be found here.

Side Dish

Salmon Cakes

These are small cakes made with salmon and either bread crumbs or mashed potatoes, then fried. They are a delicious side to eat with a full entre, and fairly simple to make with the directions here.

Dessert

Mango Sticky Rice Pudding

This is a delicious dessert. Make the rice pudding from sticky rice, then place fresh mango--in fancy balls or in simpler slices--on top of the rice. It's an uncomplicated dessert that anyone could make with some instruction.
